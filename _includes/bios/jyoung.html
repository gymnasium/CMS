Jason Young

James Young is Director of User Experience at SS&C, a financial services firm based in Boston. He collaborates with a team of designers and developers to build enterprise financial web applications, alongside internal tools to empower globally distributed software development.

He has worked as both a consultant and in-house design leader helping companies of all stages and sizes to improve their business outcomes by focusing on customers and users. He is a staunch proponent of leveraging prototyping, paired with research and testing, to inform the creation of useful and trusted products and services.
