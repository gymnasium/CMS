Jen Kramer

Jen Kramer has been teaching and performing web design for nearly 20 years. She is currently a Lecturer at Harvard University Extension School in the Master's of Liberal Arts in Digital Media Design program, where she teaches at least five courses per year on front-end web development. She's also a prolific video author, creating over 35 training courses for Lynda.com, Frontend Masters, osTraining, O'Reilly Media, and Aquent Gymnasium. She earned her MS in Internet Strategy Management from Marlboro College.
